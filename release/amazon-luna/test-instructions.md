Status: pre-authorization draft; not submitted to Amazon.

Build identity must be copied from the final immutable artifact manifest before submission. Do not provide personal developer credentials here. If a future build requires authentication, create a dedicated least-privilege reviewer account and transmit it only through the authorized Amazon console field.

## Core route

1. Launch the game and confirm the exact release, source revision, and build timestamp.
2. Select a founder, enter a founder name and company name, and select gamepad controls.
3. Advance Zach's introduction and confirm every spoken line has matching visible text.
4. Complete the shop tour or use its skip control.
5. Accept the first customer contract through the shop phone or JobLine email.
6. Order the specified certified material through NOX.
7. Accept the traveler at Planning, cut stock at the bandsaw, and complete the tool setup.
8. At the VMC, complete the true G/M-code prompts, run the machine sequence, and inspect the result.
9. Hire a qualified employee, assign a station, and verify the worker appears on the floor.
10. Save, close, relaunch, and use Continue to verify recovery.
11. Complete the Garage graduation gate and confirm the Job Shop arrival.

## Input and television checks

- Complete title-screen and pause/settings navigation without a mouse.
- Verify Xbox-compatible controller movement, interaction, run, pause, disconnect, and reconnect.
- Verify readable text and safe-area layout at typical television viewing distance.
- Verify keyboard controls remain functional where the platform exposes keyboard input.
- Treat phone QR control as optional supplemental input; certification cannot depend on a second device.

## Current disclosure

Only Chapter 1 through the Job Shop arrival is a completed playable campaign path. Later chapters shown in campaign/storybook planning are locked or development content and must not appear in product claims for this build.
