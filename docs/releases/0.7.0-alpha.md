# REINDUSTRIALIZE 0.7.0-alpha

Release channel: first playable alpha

## Playable now

- Create and name a founder and manufacturing company.
- Learn the Garage Bay from Zach through narrated story and station guidance.
- Accept customer work, order and cut stock, set tools, run true G/M-code exercises, inspect parts, hire a team, and reach the Job Shop arrival.
- Use keyboard, Xbox-compatible controller, touch controls, or the phone QR controller.
- Save locally without creating an account.

## Release improvements

- Real human-bot gameplay footage and narrated landing-page video.
- Search discovery package: crawlable robots file, sitemap, canonical metadata, favicon/app icons, web manifest, and `llms.txt`.
- Secure, disabled-by-default Supabase preparation with local saves remaining primary.
- Versioned `/release.json` for comparing the deployed build with source.
- Amazon Luna/Appstore pre-authorization submission bundle and certification checklist.
- Dedicated mobile viewport gameplay audit and regression coverage.
- Mobile gameplay now reserves the viewport for the shop, uses compact non-overlapping objective/Zach trays, keeps short-landscape tour actions visible, and replaces the cramped button grid with left-thumb movement plus separate Use and sprint controls.
- Guided-learner mode with a three-stop hands-on shop introduction, highlighted choices, forgiving setup controls, autonomous machine timing, and required inspection before shipment.
- Durable local recovery checkpoints with current, temporary, and backup save validation.
- A 17-item equipment market that grows from Garage Bay capacity upgrades into CNC turning and 5-axis machining, welding, grinding, additive manufacturing, CMM, X-ray, and a constrained factory-logistics robotics ladder.
- Every store asset now opens a complete operating profile with five comparable stats, production capacity, real setup/cycle estimates, compressed game completion time, dependencies, space/utility needs, maintenance requirements, and playable-orientation status.
- Capped robot plans start with one fixed-route Job Shop tugger, grow into department-spanning AMRs and heavy mobile platforms, and reserve supervised humanoid assistants for the American Titan Campus; each entry declares WIP route, space, staffing, safety, maintenance, and price constraints.
- A new 32-bit aerospace equipment atlas, truthful chapter/facility/space/power gates, and clear orientation labels for workflows that are not mechanically playable yet.
- Zach's four-part store walkthrough teaches bottleneck-first buying, total installation cost, process qualification, and facility-move timing; a live growth check links directly to the six-facility roadmap.
- Equipment purchases enforce growing headcount and role plans, exact cash deductions, escalating repeat-machine prices, and saved ownership; advanced systems also require maintenance coverage.
- VMC condition wear begins after the forgiving opening chapter, blocks unsafe production when critical, and requires a hired maintenance technician plus a paid lockout/diagnose/repair/test/release service.
- Founder selection now presents large profile portraits at their correct aspect ratio; small floor sprites remain reserved for gameplay movement.
- Five maintenance/facilities hires now have distinct profile art, transparent floor sprites, skill-driven assignments, role-specific conversations, and qualification-gated repair access.
- AGV, AMR, and supervised humanoid entries have transparent 8 FPS preview sprites; the humanoid also has a dedicated profile portrait and late-campus operating constraints.
- Every equipment-store type maps to a safe stopped/damaged visual; the VMC lockout uses its damaged art in live gameplay.
- Every placed machine now maps to transparent floor art for available, safely locked-out, service-in-progress, and verified-restored states; the release gate checks all machine rows, PNG alpha, technician qualifications, and return-to-service instructions.
- The Job Shop restroom is a playable facility station with a saved isolate, sign, qualified repair, sanitize, inspect, and reopen sequence.
- Consent-aware Google Analytics defaults storage to denied and allowlists only anonymous progress events, excluding names, saves, free text, and raw input.

## Honest scope

Chapter 1 through the Job Shop arrival is the current playable campaign slice. Chapters 2–6 remain development/planned content and are not represented as shipped gameplay.
