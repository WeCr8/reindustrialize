# Landing hero direction

## Positioning

REINDUSTRIALIZE is presented as a manufacturing company-building game, not a passive machining lesson. The homepage promise is: start with an empty bay, learn real production decisions, win customers, build a team, and outgrow each facility.

The national theme stays grounded in capability: production capacity, skilled people, reliable processes, resilient supply chains, and promises kept to customers. It avoids unsupported economic claims and political endorsements.

## Research translated into design

- Hadrian leads with a direct national-scale manufacturing promise and immediately explains what it builds. REINDUSTRIALIZE uses an original equivalent structure: a short industrial challenge, motion proof, and one play action.
- Anduril and Palantir use restrained navigation, oversized declarative typography, dark cinematic media, and sparse proof. The game homepage adopts those structural principles without copying their branding, language, or assets.
- Current manufacturing research frames reindustrialization as a capacity, infrastructure, technology, and workforce problem. The homepage therefore shows material, tooling, code, inspection, hiring, and facility growth instead of relying on flag imagery or slogans alone.
- High-performing game pages let motion demonstrate the product above the fold. The hero uses actual bot-recorded gameplay, not concept footage.

## Primary journey

1. See the real game moving immediately in a muted, browser-safe loop.
2. Understand the promise: build the company and outgrow the factory.
3. Select **Play film with sound** for expressive narration and captions.
4. Select **Build your company** to enter the playable opening.

## Production contract

- Composition: `HeroCampaignV7`, 1920×1080, 48 seconds.
- Footage: real human-bot gameplay source only.
- Narrator: approved non-Zach marketing voice through ElevenLabs `eleven_v3`.
- Zach remains the in-game shop teacher and business mentor.
- Audio: narration foreground, restrained industrial music at 5.5%, exact captions.
- Accessibility: autoplay muted, explicit sound control, default WebVTT track, reduced-motion poster fallback.
- Mobile: no horizontal overflow at 390 pixels; full-width actions and readable mission copy.
